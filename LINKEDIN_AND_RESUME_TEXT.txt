Fun Club Impact Analytics

Built a data analytics project to measure the educational and community impact of Fun Club, a student-led initiative supporting Azerbaijani students through English learning, technology skills, mentorship, and college preparation. Used Python, Pandas, and Matplotlib to organize and visualize student participation, volunteer hours, instructional sessions, and program growth trends.
